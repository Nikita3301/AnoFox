Photo and video sessions with a fox

A photo session with a pet fox is not only an opportunity to get great pictures or a movie with the participation of a cunning animal, but also an unforgettable experience of positive communication with a playful and funny companion. Our beautiful animals for filming and holidays are perfectly prepared for photo sessions, including those with the participation of children.
All guests must be instructed, because even an ordinary dog ​​or cat can get a little confused in the company of unfamiliar people, to say nothing of the initially wild foxes. Although all of our animals are born and raised in captivity, therefore, they have a kind and docile nature. You can choose which animal to take photos with - with a red or white fox, a graceful silver fox or a big-eared fennec baby.
